Hi there! My name is Darin. I'm a senior undergraduate student at the University of California San Diego studying Biomedical Engineering. My research focuses on machine learning and artifical intelligence in applications of healthcare.

I currently am researching the design and development of a surgical positioning platform for minimally invasive surgery under Dr. Frank Talke in the Talke Biomedical Device Lab. Concurrently, I also conduct research exploring transient changes in stress using EEG under Dr. Tzyy-Ping Jung at the Swartz Center of Computational Neuroscience.

Outside of research, I am also the President of the IEEE student branch at UC San Diego. In addition to managing club logistics, I also volunteer my time as a project mentor throughout the academic year. Some of the projects I've mentored include creating a flow sensor, building an Android app for social networking, and conducting machine learning analysis on Spotify recommended songs.
